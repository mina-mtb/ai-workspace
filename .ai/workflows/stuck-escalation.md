# Workflow: Stuck / Escalation (loop safety)
Trigger: 3 failed attempts on a task, OR the same error twice in a row, OR a hard rule would block progress.
1. STOP. Do not retry again.
2. Write what was tried + the exact blocker into the Issue and state/CURRENT.md.
3. Hand to the human (you). Wait for direction. Do not keep spending tokens.
